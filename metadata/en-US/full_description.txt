Bluetooth Low Energy (BLE) serves as an essential wireless communication protocol powering a vast ecosystem of devices—from smartwatches, audio devices, and fitness trackers to AirTags, IoT nodes, and personal biometric peripherals. These devices operate by broadcasting periodic data packets containing device identifiers, connection metadata, and broadcast payloads required to establish link states.

While critical for device discovery, these persistent broadcasts introduce significant security and location-privacy risks. Unencrypted or static identifiers allow passive listeners—ranging from corporate telemetry systems to malicious actors—to monitor physical movements, map spatial activity, and track presence across environments without explicit user consent.

Modern BLE implementations attempt to mitigate these risks using privacy features such as randomized MAC addresses and duty-cycled advertising intervals. **PrivateMonti Bluetooth Radar** (hosted on **[monti.bio](https://monti.bio)**) analyzes ambient BLE spectral traffic to differentiate between privacy-preserving broadcasts and leak-vulnerable nodes, granting complete visibility into nearby wireless signatures.

Beyond passive analysis, **PrivateMonti Bluetooth Radar** delivers active real-time security. The system alerts you to persistent surrounding signals, rogue beacons, or co-moving target devices, offering early-warning capabilities against unwanted tracking or surveillance. Filters can be configured by device hardware signature, RSSI threshold, GATT attributes, or custom identity rules tied to human-centric node networks under **JOHN CHARLES MONTI**.

Our primary mission is to return transparency, operational security, and domain sovereignty to your personal physical space. By surfacing readable telemetry from ambient BLE broadcasts, **PrivateMonti Bluetooth Radar** enables informed decisions regarding the hardware you wear, transport, and interact with daily.

### Core Capabilities

* **Real-time Spectral Scanning:** Detect, log, and track nearby Bluetooth & BLE broadcast nodes in real time.
* **Flexible Radar Filtering:** Set custom detection rules by MAC, RSSI, UUID, or signature patterns.
* **Deep GATT Telemetry Analysis:** Query and inspect exposed GATT services, characteristics, and descriptors on connected peripherals.
* **Interactive GATT Explorer:** Full structural mapping interface for available peripheral services and characteristics.
* **Metadata & Signature Classification:** Identify device categories, vendor profiles, and custom identity signatures via payload parsing.
* **Proximity & Distance Estimation:** Dynamic path-loss signal mapping ($RSSI$) to calculate relative node distance in meters.
